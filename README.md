# Target Customer Retention Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes customer retention, churn behavior, loyalty program effectiveness, customer lifetime value (CLV), and store performance.

The objective is to identify customer retention drivers and provide actionable business recommendations to improve loyalty, reduce churn, and increase long-term customer value.

---

## Business Objectives

* Analyze customer churn patterns
* Measure repeat purchase behavior
* Evaluate loyalty program effectiveness
* Calculate Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
* Assess store and channel performance
* Segment customers into churned, repeat, and high-value groups

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1 – Executive KPI Dashboard

Key metrics including Revenue, Churn Rate, Retention Rate, Repeat Rate, Total Customers and CLV.

### Page 2 – Repeat Purchase Analysis

Customer purchase frequency analysis across age groups, regions and loyalty tiers.

### Page 3 – Loyalty & Promotion Impact

Evaluation of loyalty program performance and promotional effectiveness.

### Page 4 – Store & Channel Performance Insights

Store type analysis, channel performance and retention trends.

### Page 5 – Customer Segmentation Dashboard

Segmentation of customers into Churned, Repeat and High CLV customer groups.

---

## Key Insights

* Retention Rate reached 75.5%
* Repeat Rate reached 95.5%
* Elite customers generated the highest CLV
* North region recorded the highest churn rate
* High-value customers were concentrated in specific regions
* Loyalty program participation positively influenced retention

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Prioritize retention efforts for high-value customers.

2. Improve loyalty reward engagement and redemption programs.

3. Focus retention campaigns on high-churn customer segments and regions.
